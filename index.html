About Sunkool

At Sunkool, we are dedicated to enhancing your driving experience by providing premium car window films that offer superior UV protection, heat reduction, and privacy. Our Products

Paint Protection Film

Sunkool® keep your paint looking new with a sheet of our clear protective film. It is virtually invisible once installed and will protect against light scratches and swirl marks with maintains high-glossy premium look.

Ceramic Coating

Sunkool® Ceramic Coating keeps your car paint looking new with a layer of protective ceramic coating. In addition to being easier to maintain, a car with a ceramic coating will last longer. The body panels of a ceramic coated car will be smooth and free of abrasions.

Door Edge Protection

Sunkool® Door Edge Pro is a smart car protection system that protects your car door against minor scratches and dents in tight parking spots. Our Door Edge Pro is extremely durable and long lasting.

Heat Control Window Film

Sunkool® Heat Control Window Film is a long-term solution for protecting upholstery from the sun harmful rays. Our window tint blocks out all harmful UV rays, keeping your car cooler and protect your eyes and skin. It comes with high clarity.
